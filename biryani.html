PRAWN BIRYANI(SHRIMP BIRYANI)

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g Prawns
  • 3 Onion
  • 2 Tomato
  • 3 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 100ml Coconut Milk or Curd
  • 100ml Oil
  • 1kg Rice
  • 6 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 3 tsp Tumeric
  • Salt
  • 5-10 Mint Leaves
  • Coriander Leaves
  • 4 tsp Prawn Biryani Masala
  • 2 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 2 Bay Leaf
  • 4 Cloves
  • 2 Star Anise
  • 4 Green Cardamoms
  • 2 tsp Shahi Jeera

PREPARATION FOR PRAWN BIRYANI

  1. Drain the water completely from clean prawns,add chili powder,tumeric,salt& masala powder

  2. Marinate and rub them gently for a while with the marinade.Set aside for at least 30 minutes

  3. Wash and soak rice for about 30 minutes.Drain off completely.Bring 3 to 4 cups of water to boil with salt,a bay leaf, 4 green cardamoms,1/2 tsp shahi jeera. Add rice to pot and cook until fully done but al dente.It should be grainy not mushy.

  4. Heat oil in pan add bay leaf,shahi jeera,cloves,cardamom,star anise and cinnamon> use just a inch of cinnamon

  5. When begin to splutter, add onion and cook until brown or golden

  6. Add ginger garlic paste and saute till if smells good

  7. Add chopped tomatoes,mint,coriander leaves cook until soft

  8. Add tumeric,red chilli powder,biryani masala,coriander powder

  9. cook until oil begins to seperate

  10. Add marinated prawns

  11. Fry for a minute

  12. Pour slightly thick coconut milk and stir or add curd/yogurt

  13. Cook until gravy thickens and prawns are cooked properly.The curled up "C" shape indicates the prawns are cooked properly

  14. Layer the biryani add rice layer

  15. next gravy layer

  16. Add coriander leaves and mint add saffron

  17. Cover with foil and cook for 15 to 20 min on low flame

  18. Slow cooking method where steam is trapped in pot and flavours are properly incubated in the biryani.

  19. Serve with raita
